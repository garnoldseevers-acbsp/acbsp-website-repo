Our Partnerships

PRME Steering Committee

ACBSP is one of 10 global organizations serving on the Principles for Responsible Management (PRME) Steering Committee. The mission of PRME is to transform management education, research, and thought leadership globally by providing the Principles for Responsible Management Education framework, developing learning communities, and promoting awareness about the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals.

The Alliance for Performance Excellence

A non-profit organization dedicated to enhancing the growth and sustainability of the Baldrige Foundation and the National Baldrige Program, noting “ACBSP is unique among business school accreditation associations in its long-standing and explicit use of the Baldrige Framework for Performance Excellence as the foundation of its accreditation standards and criteria and review process.”

Global Academy of Finance and Management (GAFM)

For more than 10 years, ACBSP and GAFM have benefited from a mutual articulation agreement. The current agreement between ACBSP and GAFM International Board of Standards states: All ACBSP baccalaureate/graduate degree schools that have attained accredited status will be officially recognized by the GAFM with an educational and degree path toward certification eligibility. ACBSP accredited business school programs are recognized as GAFM eligible programs for the GAFM Master Financial Planner (MFP) designation and other credentials listed at GAFM.org.

MyCOS

Founded in 2006, MyCOS is China’s first and most trusted firm specialized in higher education management consulting. We provide data-driven consulting to improve educational processes and outcomes for over 1,000 colleges and universities across China, and host China's largest forum on higher education management. Within China, we have robust research collaborations with universities including Tsinghua University, Peking University and Beijing Normal University; internationally, we have collaborated with the World Bank, the OECD, the Ford Foundation, and the Asian Development Bank. MyCOS advocates for higher education best practices through our in-house MyCOS Research magazine and the annual Chinese College Graduates’ Employment Annual Report, which uses MyCOS’ powerful data sets to provide insight into the state of college graduate employment in China.
