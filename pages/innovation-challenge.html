The Challenge

What can we do to enhance the ACBSP brand worldwide?

The ACBSP Board of Directors, led by Board Chair Dr. Alejandro Cheyne, has identified a challenge for ACBSP member faculty and their students to solve. We need your innovative ideas. Submit a 5-minute video with your proposal. Faculty, engage your students. Collaboration leads to innovation!

Solution

Your innovative ideas. Submit a video (5 minutes in length or less) with your proposal. Faculty, engage your students. Collaboration leads to innovation!

How to Apply

Create a group of maximum 5 people where students and teachers are included.

Send the application document with the information and participants, the university and a video in English of maximum 5 minutes in which the proposal is explained.

An explanatory document of the proposal of maximum 10 pages can be attached. (optional)

Selection Criteria

  • Leadership, Charisma, Knowledge, Preparation and Presentation Order.
  • Diagnosis, Solution Quality and Innovation.

Selection Process

  1. Selection of the 5 best videos with a group of juries.
  2. Virtual voting to choose the best 3 videos.
  3. Board of Directors chooses the winner with the 3 finalists.
