Submit a Professional Article for Publication

Do you consider yourself a subject-matter expert in the business education or higher ed industries? Submit an entry to be featured on, either, the print or online version of ACBSP Impact.

Here are some basic parameters for entries:

  • Please provide your article in a word document, Times New Roman font, 12 pt.
  • Please thoroughly discuss why the subject matter is important. What can readers gain from the content? Your entry must be in article format. Formal papers or Abstracts will not be accepted for the blog.
  • Please do not exceed 1500 words. Articles surpassing this word count will be disqualified for the blog, but may still be published in the ACBSP Update.
  • If you wish to submit complimentary photos for your article, please email them separately. Including images in the word document is NOT ACCEPTABLE, this distorts the image resolution.
