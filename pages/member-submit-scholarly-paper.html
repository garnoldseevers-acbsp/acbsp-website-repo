Publishing Opportunities with ACBSP

Manuscripts must adhere to the Publication's theme.

The theme provides a dialogue forum that advances teaching excellence through research and discussion, with particular emphasis on the practical applications of ideas that contribute to the theme.

Manuscripts shall be submitted in the following format:

  • Single-spaced on 8.5" x 11" page
  • Times New Roman font
  • Main text - 12 point font
  • Major headings - 14 point font
  • Manuscripts should be approximately 5,000 â€“ 7,000 words, including all graphs, figures, and references
  • Authors must follow APA Formatting and Style Guide Manual, 6th Edition

Manuscripts must be written in English.

Non-native speakers are encouraged to seek assistance from a colleague who is proficient in English to proofread the paper.

Manuscripts for Review must be submitted through Scholastica.

The TJB requires all manuscripts for review and publication to be submitted electronically -- via our online submission system -- Manuscripts submitted via any other method will not be accepted or considered.
