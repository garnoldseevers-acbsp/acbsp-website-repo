Awards

Teaching Excellence Award

ACBSP is the only specialized accrediting body for business schools that presents an award recognizing excellence in teaching that is open to the entire membership. The International Teaching Excellence Award recipients will be honored at the "Salute to Regions" luncheon at the ACBSP Annual Conference, where they will each be presented with an engraved crystal award and a check for $500.

Best of Regions

The International Best of Regions Excellence Award was introduced in 2012 as a way to honor exceptional presentations given by members with the ACBSP Annual Conference theme. During the Salute to Regions Luncheon on Sunday, one of the Best of the Region presentations will receive the International Best of Regions Excellence Award. The recipient presenter(s) will receive a crystal award as well as a $500 check.

Educational Impact Award

The ACBSP Educational Impact Award recognizes the most effective and influential educational initiatives by an organization in the host city of the ACBSP Annual Conference, in support of learning and education in primary, secondary, and post-secondary institutions and the community. The ACBSP Educational Impact Award is presented annually. The recipient is recognized at the ACBSP Annual Conference in June.

Student Showcase

Each year, one student presentation will be selected to represent their school and region and present at the ACBSP Annual Conference. This is an excellent professional development opportunity for students at all levels of higher business education, and a valuable addition to a résumé.

Student Leadership Award

The ACBSP Student Leadership Award was created in order to recognize student leadership. No other business school accrediting body has an award like it. Once presented, the student may use this recognition on a resume and be honored at a graduation or honor awards assembly. The availability of a perpetual plaque in the wall of the business school adds value for the student long after departure from the college or university campus and is visible evidence to all visitors that the business unit supports leadership within the student body.
