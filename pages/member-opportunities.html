Participate in PhD research on negotiator's behavior

Prof. Ana Tereza Spinola of FGV Brasil is conducting a research on the correlation between the psychological profile of individuals and their negotiating profile, that is, how much the negotiator´s profile is determined by his psychological profile. This research will serve as the basis for the conclusion of the doctorate of Prof. Ana Tereza at the Rennes School of Business in France.
